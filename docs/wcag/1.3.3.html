WCAG 2.2 · Level A

1.3.3 Sensory Characteristics

Success Criterion

Instructions provided for understanding and operating content do not rely solely on sensory characteristics of components such as shape, color, size, visual location, orientation, or sound. Note For requirements related to color, refer to Guideline 1.4.

Understanding 1.3.3 on W3C

Sufficient techniques

  • G96 Providing textual identification of items that otherwise rely only on sensory information to be understood

Documented failures anti-patterns

  • F14 Failure of Success Criterion 1.3.3 due to identifying content only by its shape or location
  • F26 Failure of Success Criterion 1.3.3 due to using a graphical symbol alone to convey information
